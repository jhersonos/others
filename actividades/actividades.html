We are travelers who design experiences for travelers like us. We look for people to enjoy and discover new landscapes. No hassles, no pressures and enough freedom to explore everything. We specialize in short routes within Lima and we have a staff of professionals who love what they do.
English | Spanish

What we will do

We will visit one of the most beautiful archaeological sanctuaries in Lima. In this sanctuary you can see the best sunrise of the city because you will be surrounded by a mattress denims and the sun rising above the Andes.
What i’ll Provide
  • - Transport from Lima - Chosica - San Pedro
  • - Tourist guide, Entrance ticket (foreigners must add 10 soles)
  • - 02 lunches (vegetarian option)
  • - Assistance during the trip
  • - Campfire night and excursions
notes
Bring your own bathing suit and any beach necessities. Whether that be special foods and drinks. If you have your own wetsuit. Always bring sunscreen and warm clothing. Pants and jacket recommended.
where we will meet
Cruce del Jr. Chota con Paseo Colon (Altura de la Plaza Bolognesi)
where we will be
San Pedro de Casta, town of Huarochiri known for its innumerable traditions such as the feast of water or horse races. The Laguna Cachu Cachu and the Laguna Huacracocha. The monument to humanity, a huge rock where different faces of cultures of the world are visualized. The hut of Dr. Daniel Ruzo, researcher of Markahuasi who spent 9 years exploring it. Pre-Inca remains and a lot of shapes in the rock.

Welcome to Lima
When fog bundles its colonial facades and high rises, Lima's enchantments come across as all too subtle. After Cairo, this sprawling metropolis is the second-driest world capital, rising above a long coastline of crumbling cliffs. To enjoy it, climb on the wave of chaos that spans from high-rise condos built alongside pre-Columbian temples
